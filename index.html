Time

September 2021
A project that explores time through units and increments. What better way to show time passing than the ever-changing sky? I used a modular system of triangles to show the sky on different dates, sunlight through a window, and the daylight cycle. I used and broke the grid in the form of a gif, poster, and booklet. Made in Illustrator and Blender.
