About me

Hi I'm Sara!
I'm a graphic design student at MTSU, graduating in 2024. I was born in Egypt & raised in Nashville. I want to focus my career on conceptual illustration and web design. I have experience in Illustrator, Photoshop, Indesign, HTML, CSS, and JS. I am self taught in many softwares and mediums, and I've earned the Adobe Illustrator certification on Linkedin Learning.
I have an instagram where i post things that are more illustration and less design.

Time

September 2021
A project that explores time through units and increments. What better way to show time passing than the ever-changing sky? I used a modular system of triangles to show the sky on different dates, sunlight through a window, and the daylight cycle. I used and broke the grid in the form of a gif, poster, and booklet. Made in Illustrator and Blender.
